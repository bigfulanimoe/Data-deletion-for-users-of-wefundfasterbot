Data Deletion Instructions – WeFundFasterBot

If you would like to request the deletion of your data collected by the Facebook Messenger App WeFundFasterBot, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to our data officer at:

bigolatunde82@gmail.com

Use the subject line: “Data Deletion Request” and include the Facebook account associated with your interaction with the bot.

Once received, your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days and you will receive a confirmation email.

If this request came from Facebook’s automated data deletion callback, no action is required on your part—your data will be handled in accordance with Meta’s platform rules.
